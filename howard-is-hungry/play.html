HOWARD IS HUNGRY!

The voracious sea turtle Howard has found a popular tourist-infested harbor.

Delicious morsels of food are frequently tossed overboard -- but so are fishing lines with painful hooks!

1P) Try to eat as much food as you can before getting caught three times.

2P) Compete with Howard's sister Henrietta to eat more food in a short period of time;

points increase over time, but get caught and you'll lose (half) your lunch!

Controls: Arrow keys (Green Howard), WASD (Red Henrietta)
